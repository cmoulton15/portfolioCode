There are a lot of things that go into building a website. To best represent your business, Moulton Hot Design focuses on a variety of important design principles. These principles are based around helping viewers see the most useful information about your business in an engaging way and then being guided to take the next step if they are interested.

Another big part of the design of your website is the functionality (user-focused experience) and discover-ability (search engine optimization), both of which we can help with. Take a look at some of these principles you will benefit from by working with Moulton Hot Design. Get Your Website »

Brand

Your brand as a business is vitally important. The color and feel of your site will help viewers stay focused in an information-saturated world. A distinctive brand will help people remember your business more easily.

Content

The content on a website has always been and still is king. This content should reflect the ideals and services of your business accurately and be laid out in an easy to follow format for the most pleasant experience.

Call To Action

Every person who visits your site should know what action to take. For the best UX, a quality website needs to have clear Calls to Action for viewers to know where to go next depending on their level of interest.

Responsiveness

In today's world, more people are looking at websites on their phones than any other medium. To be relevant on the web, you need a website to be responsive across different screen sizes to accommodate all devices.

Connection

A personal connection with your customers will help them more fully trust your business. It's important to connect potential customers to your Social Media platforms to give them updates and build connection.

SEO

Without being discoverable, your website means nothing. A large part of SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of building your website in a way that helps people find it when they search your services on Google.
