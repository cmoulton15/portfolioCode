Perhaps the most important visual for your target audience to remember is your logo. The colors used and the general look of your logo should fit your business theme in a creative and interesting way.

Here at Moulton Hot Design, we provide your logo in a variety of formats so that you can print, display, or present your logo at a high quality resolution for most mediums. We'll also provide you with a theme to help you continue branding for your business in the future. Get Your Logo »

Brand

Your logo should be the defining point of your brand, evoking emotions in viewers that match your services. We build logos so that they represent your business in the most memorable way.

Formats

Logos need to be adaptable to many different mediums. Moulton Hot Design can provide you with multiple formats, namely raster and vector, so that you can display your logo on all mediums.

Theme

With your logo, we include a theme that you can refer to in the future with your business. This theme will help you keep your font and color branding consistent across other mediums.
