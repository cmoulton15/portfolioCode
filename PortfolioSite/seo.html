It is of vital importance that your website is easy to find on the internet. Without having a quality website that provides relevant information to viewers, both in search engines and on your actual site, your business may suffer from being difficult to find.

Moulton Hot Design builds websites in a way that meets the standards of search engines and viewers. We do this in the most ethical way and strictly avoid black hat tactics (ways of gaming the system). There are principles to follow with SEO that can set your website up with a solid foundation, optimized for search on the web. We stick to those principles. Get Your Site Optimized »

Keywords

You want your site optimized with certain keywords so that people can find you when and where they need you. These keywords should be location and service specific, make you stand out, and avoid competing with popular keywords.

Meta Data

A website's meta data plays a big roll in making your site appear in the SERP (Search Engine Results Page) effectively and to search engine standards. Optimizing meta data will improve your rankings and your rapport.

Copywriting

Content is still king on the web. Moulton Hot Design can help build your website with the right amount of engaging text, built around your keywords. This will keep your content relevant to viewers and help improve your search rankings.

Analytics

It's important to know how your website is doing and what viewers are doing when they get there. We can integrate Google Analytics with your Wordpress editor to provide these insights - or, if you prefer, help set up an Analytics account.
